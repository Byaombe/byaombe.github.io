Why Are You Dating that person?

  • Relationship
December 1st, 2017

You're probably dating now, or you've had a significant other, but have you vever asked yourself "why am I dating this persons?" this is the question everyone should ask themselves while in a relationship. In fact before dating someone you should ask yourself that question. Your answer to that question could either build someone or break them.


I remember when a guy asked me on a date, my first question was always, "Do I see myself marying this guy?, Can we make it?, Am I interested in a long term relationship?" these where my thoughts, and if any of the answers were no, then the answer was always no to the guy, didnt care who they were. I'm not there to waste anyone's life, because while they're with me, they could've found someone who's their perfect match and could possible build with them while I'm not.


No, it's not all about fun when in teh end someone gets hurt because of your carelessness. its not "Just fun" there's nothing fun about that.
